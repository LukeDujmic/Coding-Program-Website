Association

West-Mec Northeast Coding is associated with the Future Business Leaders of America (FBLA) program. Coding is currently the only program in the Northeast Campus to be involved in FBLA.

Class Officers

The Coding program gives students the opportunity to become apart of the FBLA program by being officers. All students are automatically apart of FBLA in the class by being members. Students can go further and become officers who manage certain aspects of the classroom and events as a whole.
