Class Overview

The Coding class at the WestMec Northeast Campus offers students the highest level of capabilities. It offers an interactive experience in which the students build their skills in coding from the very basics all the way to complex understanding, which will give students the fullest understanding. This two-year program leads students onto the career of a software developer. The program introduces students to website design, and leads them into scripting throughout the following semesters. And by the end of the two-year program, students will be javascript certified.
