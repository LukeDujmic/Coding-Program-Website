Future Events

Students will participate in FBLA related events throughout the year, based on the competitive events.

Comepitive Event Trip in November

In the month of November, students who have signed up for the competitive event trip will participate in multiple activites, based on what they have tested into, in which the students will enjoy their time in ASU Polytechnic in Tempe. Students will also travel to Tuscon for state competition as the events go further.

Summer Vacation Trip (Undetermined Location)

During the summer break following the first year of Coding class, students will travel to a destination for a week. This destination is to be determined by the students by vote, and approved by staff. The trip will allow students to enjoy themselves with their peers and their instructor, as well as spend quality time doing activities determined by adults.
