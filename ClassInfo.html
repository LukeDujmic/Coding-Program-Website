Schedule

Students will either end up in Session 1 or Session 2 for coding class. The two sessions, however, will merge during the second year of the program.

Session 1: 1:00 - 3:30
Session 2: 4:00 - 6:30

During those class periods, students will be given a 15 minutes break period around the middle of the class period (typically at 2:15). Within this time, students can go on their devices and even exit the classroom to visit the student center or other locations.

Tardies and Absences

West-MEC Northeast campus has set policies on arriving in the school campus. For the coding class, students are permitted to enter the classroom at 12:55, and the class initiates at 1:00. If students are not within the classroom by 1:00, and they enter past this time, they will be marked with a tardy, but still as present. When a student is not able to make it into the classroom before that time, they will need to go to the front desk downstairs and request a pass. If a student happens to be in the class for less than half the class period, then they will be marked be counted as absent.

When a student is counted as present for all days of the month, they will be given a paper that can be used for a reward raffle at the end of the semester/year.
