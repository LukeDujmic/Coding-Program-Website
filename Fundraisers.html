Fundraising and Drives

To raise money for events, the Coding program participates in fundraisers and drives.

Shoe Drive

The shoe drive was initiated to allow the Coding class to participate in competitive events, as they require support. For the shoe drive, students across the campus must gather unused shoes to be packaged in bags. These bags of shoes will be sent to those in need in Africa.

As of 10/28/19, the class has gathered 554 pairs of shoes!
